Leave Policy

Employees receive 12 casual leaves per year.

Unused leaves cannot be carried forward.

Manager approval is required for planned leave.


Medical Insurance

Employees become eligible after 90 days.

Family coverage is available.


Work From Home

Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week.

Manager approval may be required.


Travel Policy

Travel expenses must be approved by the manager.

Original receipts are required.
